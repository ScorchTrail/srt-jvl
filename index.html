Meet Javier

A father, a craftsman,
a Scottsdale neighbor.

Javier started this company in 2009 with one truck, a hand-drawn portfolio, and a belief that a backyard should feel like a hug at the end of a hard day. Fifteen years later, his three kids work alongside him.

We design for the moments most landscapers forget: the morning coffee under the ramada, the kids running barefoot on cool pavers... That's the work we love.

  • Born and raised in the Valley — we know our soil, our sun, our water.
  • Bilingual crews who treat your home with the respect we'd give our abuela's.
  • 2-year plant health guarantee on every install.
