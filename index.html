Meet Javier

A father, a landscaper,
a Scottsdale neighbor.

Javier started this journey 15 years ago with just one truck, a lawnmower, and a belief that a backyard should feel like a warm hug at the end of a hard day. Today, his children work right alongside him.

We care for the spaces where life happens: the quiet morning coffee overlooking a freshly cut lawn, and the kids playing safely in the yard. That's the work we pour our hearts into.

  • Born and raised in the Valley—we intimately understand our soil, our sun, and our weeds.
  • Bilingual crews who treat your home with the exact same love and respect we'd give our own abuela's yard.
  • A 2-year plant health guarantee on every new planting, because we treat your yard like our own.
